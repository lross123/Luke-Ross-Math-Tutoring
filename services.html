Services & Pricing

I offer comprehensive maths tuition for students at GCSE, A‑Level and university level. Sessions are available as one‑to‑one lessons either online via video call or in person in the Crowthorne area. All tuition begins with a free consultation to understand your goals and plan a tailored programme.

GCSE Maths

Covering the full GCSE curriculum across all exam boards, including Foundation and Higher tiers. Topics range from algebra and geometry to statistics and trigonometry. Resources include revision guides, past papers and bespoke practice questions.

£40 per hour

A‑Level Maths & Further Maths

Support for AS and A‑Level Mathematics as well as Further Mathematics modules. I help students master core concepts such as calculus, mechanics, statistics and pure mathematics, with structured exam preparation and plenty of guided practice.

£50 per hour

Degree‑Level Modules

Assistance with university maths modules including linear algebra, real and complex analysis, probability and stochastic processes, numerical methods and more. As degree courses can vary considerably, please get in touch with details of your module so I can confirm availability and provide a tailored quote.
